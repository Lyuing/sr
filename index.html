About Us

  • Baoji Longda Jiaye Industry and Trade Co., Ltd. was established in 2019 and is located in Baoji City, Shanxi Province, Titanium Valley, China. Relying on the local geographical advantages, the company has developed into an innovative enterprise integrating production and trade. The company specializes in the production of titanium alloy materials and tungsten, molybdenum, tantalum, niobium, nickel, zirconium and other rare metals. Our products include bar, plate, pipe, wire, powder, foil, fasteners and customized machining parts. According to GB/T, ASTM/B, ASME SB, AMS, DIN, JIS and other standards of processing. The products are widely used in aerospace, medical equipment, automobile, metallurgy, electronics, ceramics and petrochemical industries. Products have been exported to the United States, Canada, Brazil, Japan, South Korea, Germany, Britain, Australia, Southeast Asia and other countries and regions. We rely on good quality and service to win the majority of customers.
